Case Study: Atomic Improv

Objective

Atomic Improv is a local comedy duo that has been preforming all over the world for over 30 years. They hadn't had their website updated in several years and much of the content was out of date.

The Design

In order to nail down the design we ran through a design sprint, took a lot of feedback from the client and ended up settling on a dark theme with details like a brick wall background and neon light elements to nod to the years the client spent doing stand up comedy at clubs.

The Development

The client was already familiar with the Wordpress ecosystem so we chose to keep that as a base to build on. I created a custom theme using php and sass.

I was also responsible for checking accessability, that it met WC3 standards and the creation of a styleguide for the client.
